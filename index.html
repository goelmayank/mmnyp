The Youth Parliament is a platform for the youth to speak up and make an impact on a change agenda for the country. To take on the policy makers and highlight the need for new thinking that will take the country forward. The youth parliament will be a debate between the best debaters and policy makers and opinion makers. The platform will inspire the youth to express their views in an organised way. It will also allow for the emergence of bright future leaders. The target is to engage youth in a participative dialogue in a format that has never been done before. Youth have become deeply opinionated but at times are frustrated with the lack of opportunity to get their voice and opinion heard. The youth parliament is a platform for them to step forward and question policy and the people who have been formulating the policy. To highlight to the establishment that they are not satisfied with the status quo and that they have the ideas and an agenda for the change.
1st MAHAMANA MALVIYA NATIONAL YOUTH PARLIAMENT 2017, three days conference in the premises of Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi which is scheduled to take place from 17th to 19th February 2017. Youth Parliament is a platform for the youth to debate and take on the policy making and highlights the need for new thinking that will take the country forward. The Organising Committee of MMNYP is glad to hold 5 National Committees and 2 International Committees in the first edition. All the agendas have been decided by the secretariat members with consultation with the experienced executive board. The organising team of the MMNYP are mainly students of Banaras Hindu University, who dream to take the message of Pt. Malviya ji of team work and indulgence of youth in integration of the nation and raise voices on issues which are unsocial or which requires the attention of the people. MMNYP is just a step forward and the youth of BHU would keep on working to bring more of such events in future. We as a team urge you to come join us and make this event a successful platform for fruitful debate.
